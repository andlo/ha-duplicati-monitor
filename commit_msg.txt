Fix regression: missing max_columns/column_span made the whole dashboard narrow

Simplifying to one section in the previous commit dropped the view's
max_columns setting and never gave that lone section a column_span -
without it, a section claims only one "slot" out of the up-to-4 the
view could show side by side, and does NOT expand to fill the row
just because there's nothing next to it. Result: the entire dashboard
content was squeezed into roughly a quarter of the page width, per
andlo's screenshot.

Added max_columns: 4 at the view level and column_span: 4 on the
section, so it explicitly claims the full row.
