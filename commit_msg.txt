v0.3.6: allow permanently deleting a job's device (closes issue #3)

andlo asked how to remove a retired test job ("fedora - TEST") -
turned out there was no way to do it. Deleting the device via
Settings > Devices & Services alone doesn't help: since v0.2.0
persists every known job so entities survive restarts, the device
would just come back on the next restart or next report.

Added async_remove_config_entry_device - the official Home Assistant
hook for exactly this (multi-device integrations letting users remove
individual devices via the UI's trash icon). Purges the job from our
own storage (jobs + history) before returning True to let HA proceed
with the actual removal, so "delete device" now actually means delete.

Documented in a new README section, since silently-reappearing devices
after a "successful" deletion would be a confusing failure mode
otherwise.

Covered by a new test: create a job, delete its device through the
same hook HA calls, simulate a restart, assert it doesn't come back
and the sensor is genuinely gone.
